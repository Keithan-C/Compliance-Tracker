# EU AI Act Article 12/19 Compliance Tracker

**Interactive 46-item checklist for automotive ADAS and infrastructure teams preparing for EU AI Act enforcement (August 2, 2026).**

Live Demo: `https://keithan-c.github.io/ai-act-article-12-19-checklist/`

---

## Features

✅ **46 compliance items** across Article 4, 12, 19, and Audit Readiness  
✅ **Progress auto-saves** in browser (localStorage)  
✅ **Flag blockers** to track what's holding you back  
✅ **Export status** as plain text for team sharing  
✅ **Mobile responsive** — works on any device  
✅ **Print/PDF export** with clean formatting  

---

## What's Included

### Section A: Article 4 — AI Literacy & Team Readiness (8 items)
- Staff training and awareness
- High-risk AI classification
- Documentation register

### Section B: Article 12 — Logging Capability (20 items)
- Logging architecture
- Data capture requirements
- Logging schemas
- Retrieval capability

### Section C: Article 19 — Log Control (14 items)
- Retention policies
- Access control
- Integrity protection

### Section D: Audit Readiness (10 items)
- Core documentation
- Team readiness
- Mock audit preparation

---

## How to Use

1. Open the tracker in your browser
2. Click items to check them off as completed
3. Click the flag button (⚑) to mark blockers
4. Progress saves automatically
5. Use "Copy Status" to export progress summary
6. Use "Print/PDF" to generate printable version

---

## Technical Details

**Technology Stack:**
- Single HTML file (no dependencies)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Browser localStorage for persistence
- CSS animations and responsive design

**Data Storage:**
- All data stored locally in your browser
- No server, no database, no data collection
- Privacy-first design — your data stays on your device

**Browser Compatibility:**
- Chrome/Edge (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Mobile browsers (iOS/Android)
